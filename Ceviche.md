Notes: on tortitas

##  Ingredients (2 persons)

* [ ] **White fish (isaki / mahi-mahi / buri)** — **180–200 g**
* [ ] **Lime or shīkwāsā juice** — **3–4 tbsp** *(≈ 45–60 ml)*
* [ ] **Fine sea salt** — **½ tsp**
* [ ] **Red onion (very thin)** — **2 tbsp**
* [ ] **Cucumber or daikon (small dice)** — **2 tbsp**
* [ ] **Mango (small dice)** — **2 tbsp**
* [ ] **Fresh ginger (juice only)** — **¼ tsp**
* [ ] **Mild chili (optional)** — **2–3 thin slices**
* [ ] **Roasted canchitas** — **2 tbsp**
* [ ] **Sesame oil (optional)** — **2 drops**
* [ ] **Citrus zest** — **pinch**


## Method

1. Cube fish (≈1.5 cm), keep cold
2. Salt fish → **2 min**
3. Add citrus (just to coat) → **3 min**
4. Gently fold in onion + cucumber
5. Add mango, ginger, chili
6. Taste → adjust salt only
7. Plate, top with canchitas + zest
8. Eat immediately
