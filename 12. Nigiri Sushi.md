

#### Rice quantity (2 servings)
- [ ] Japanese short-grain rice — 180 g  
- [ ] Water — same amount as rice  
- [ ] Seasoned rice vinegar — 30 ml  

This yields **~450–480 g cooked rice**, enough for **24–28 nigiri**.

---

### Technique

1. Rinse rice until water runs mostly clear.
2. Drain well, cook in rice cooker on white-rice setting.
3. Rest 10 minutes with lid closed.
4. Transfer to a wide bowl.
5. Add vinegar gradually.
6. Mix with slicing + lifting motion, fanning gently.
7. Cover with a damp cloth; **keep warm**.

---

### Shaping nigiri

- **Rice per nigiri:** 15–18 g

1. Lightly wet hands.
2. Take rice, form a loose oval.
3. Rotate 90° between fingers and palm.
4. Press gently on the sides only.
5. Shape should be slightly angled and airy.

Rice should hold together but break apart easily when eaten.
