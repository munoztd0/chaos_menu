### Ingredients
- [ ] Baby artichokes (carciofini) — 600 g
- [ ] Olive oil — 70 ml
- [ ] Garlic — 2 cloves, thinly sliced
- [ ] Chili flakes — ½–1 tsp
- [ ] Lemon — 1 (juice only)
- [ ] Dry white wine — 60 ml

---

### Preparation
1. Clean artichokes: remove tough outer leaves, trim stems, halve lengthwise. Keep in lemon water.
2. Heat olive oil gently, add garlic and chili flakes; cook until fragrant.
3. Add drained artichokes, cut side down. Cook gently until lightly golden.
4. Deglaze with white wine, reduce briefly.
5. Cover and cook on low until tender.
6. Finish with a little lemon juice.
