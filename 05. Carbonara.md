
### Ingredients (2 servings)

- [ ] Spaghettoni — 200 g
- [ ] Guanciale — 120 g, cut into 1 cm dice
- [ ] Eggs — 3 (use 2 yolks)
- [ ] Parmigiano Reggiano — 40 g, finely grated
- [ ] Pecorino Romano — 40 g, finely grated
- [ ] Whole black peppercorns — 2 tsp

---

### Preparation
1. Cut guanciale into 1 cm dice. Render slowly; keep the fat so the guanciale stays crunchy.
2. Toast peppercorns until they start to jump, then grind.
3. In a bowl over a bain-marie, mix eggs, Parmigiano, Pecorino, ground pepper, 2 tbsp guanciale fat, and a small splash of cooking water.
4. Cook spaghettoni until al dente.
5. Add pasta to guanciale, toss.
6. Off heat, add egg mixture and combine quickly.
7. Plate using a fork and spoon.
8. Finish with extra pepper and Parmigiano.
