### Ingredients
- [ ] Spinach — 300 g
- [ ] Sesame seeds — 4 tbsp
- [ ] Soy sauce — 1 tbsp
- [ ] Sugar — ¾ tbsp
- [ ] Dashi powder — 1 tsp
- [ ] Water — 1 tbsp

---

### Preparation
1. Blanch spinach, stems first, then leaves. Drain and cool.
2. Toast sesame seeds 5–6 minutes until golden.
3. Crush seeds in a mortar to a coarse, sand-like texture.
4. Mix crushed seeds with soy sauce, sugar, dashi powder, and water.
5. Combine with spinach. Serve cold.
