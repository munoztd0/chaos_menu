


### Ingredients (2 pers)

#### Dahl
- [ ] Red lentils — 180 g
- [ ] Water — 600 ml
- [ ] Onion — 120 g, finely chopped
- [ ] Garlic — 2 cloves (10 g), minced
- [ ] Fresh ginger — 10 g, grated (optional)
- [ ] Ground turmeric — 1 tsp
- [ ] Ground cumin — 1 tsp
- [ ] Chili flakes or powder — ½–1 tsp
- [ ] Neutral or coconut oil — 15 g
- [ ] Salt — to taste
- [ ] Lemon or lime juice — to taste

#### Red cabbage & dill slaw (no apples)
- [ ] Red cabbage — 250 g, thinly sliced
- [ ] Red onion — 50 g, very thinly sliced
- [ ] Fresh dill — 10 g, finely chopped
- [ ] Lemon zest — from ½ lemon (optional)
- [ ] Salt — to taste
- [ ] Black pepper — to taste

![Alt text](https://www.everydayhealthyrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/red-cabbage-slaw-apple.jpg)

#### Slaw dressing
- [ ] Extra-virgin olive oil — 30 ml
- [ ] Honey or maple syrup — 10 g
- [ ] Cider vinegar — 15 ml
- [ ] Mustard (Dijon or mild) — 1 tsp


#### Rice
- [ ] 1 cup

---

### Preparation

#### Dahl
1. Simmer lentils, water, turmeric, and a pinch of salt for 12–15 min until soft and creamy.
2. Heat oil in a pan. Cook onion with a pinch of salt until soft. Add garlic, ginger, cumin, and chili; cook 30 seconds.
3. Add mixture to lentils. Simmer 5 min. Adjust salt and thickness.
4. Finish with lemon or lime juice.

#### Slaw
1. Whisk dressing ingredients with half the dill.
2. Combine cabbage and onion in a large bowl.
3. Add dressing, remaining dill, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
4. Gently lift and fold. Rest 10–15 min before serving.
5. Serve with rice
