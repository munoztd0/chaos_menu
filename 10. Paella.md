### Ingredients (4 people)

- [ ] Bomba or Calasparra rice — 360 g
- [ ] Broth (fish or chicken) — 900 ml
- [ ] Shrimp — 400 g
- [ ] Squid — 400 g, sliced
- [ ] Mussels — 800 g
- [ ] Canned tomato — 300 g, crushed
- [ ] Green peas — 200 g
- [ ] Bell pepper — 2 medium (300 g), sliced
- [ ] Chicken thighs — 600 g, bite-size (optional)
- [ ] Olive oil — 80 ml
- [ ] Paprika — 2 tsp
- [ ] Saffron — a good pinch

---

### Pan
- **Paella pan diameter:** 38–40 cm  
- Rice should form a thin, even layer (single grain depth) for proper **socarrat**

---

### Preparation
1. Heat olive oil in the paella pan. Sear chicken (if using); remove.
2. Sauté bell pepper, then squid briefly.
3. Add crushed tomato, paprika, saffron; cook until reduced.
4. Add rice, stir to coat evenly.
5. Pour in hot broth evenly over the pan.
6. Arrange chicken back in, then shrimp, mussels, and peas.
7. Cook uncovered on medium-high 8–10 min, then lower heat 8–10 min.
8. Increase heat for the final 1–2 min to form the toasted bottom.
