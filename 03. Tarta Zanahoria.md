## Grandma Lucha’s Carrot Cake

### Ingredients
- [ ] All-purpose flour — 290 g
- [ ] Sugar — 500 g
- [ ] Neutral cooking oil (not olive oil) — 300 ml
- [ ] Milk — 85 ml
- [ ] Baking powder — 2 tsp
- [ ] Ground cinnamon — 2 tsp
- [ ] Baking soda — 1 tsp
- [ ] Salt — ½ tsp
- [ ] Eggs — 4
- [ ] Walnuts or pecans — 100 g (≈ 1 cup), chopped
- [ ] Carrots — 300 g (≈ 3 cups), peeled and grated

---

### Preparation
1. Using a mixer, combine in this order: sugar, oil, eggs, milk.
2. Add flour gradually, then cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix until smooth.
3. Fold in grated carrots and chopped nuts.
4. Pour into a greased baking pan and dust lightly with flour.
5. Bake at **180 °C** for **40–60 minutes**.
6. Check doneness with a toothpick inserted in the center; it should come out dry.
