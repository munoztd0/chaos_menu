### Ingredients
- [ ] Prepared Lebanese hummus with tahini — 400 g
- [ ] Lime — 1 (juice)
- [ ] Extra-virgin olive oil — 40 ml
- [ ] Za’atar — 1–2 tsp
- [ ] Paprika — 1 tsp

---

### Preparation
1. Transfer hummus to a shallow bowl.
2. Smooth the surface with the back of a spoon, creating a wide swirl.
3. Drizzle lime juice evenly over the hummus.
4. Pour olive oil into the swirl.
5. Sprinkle za’atar and paprika on top.
6. Serve at room temperature.
