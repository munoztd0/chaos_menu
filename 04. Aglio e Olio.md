
### Ingredients (2 servings)

- [ ] Linguine — 200 g
- [ ] Extra-virgin olive oil — 60 ml
- [ ] Garlic — 4 cloves, thinly sliced
- [ ] Chili flakes — ½–1 tsp
- [ ] Fresh parsley — 10 g, finely chopped
- [ ] Parmigiano Reggiano — 30 g, finely grated


---

### Preparation
1. Boil linguine in well-salted water for **3 minutes**. Drain, **reserve water**.
2. Toss pasta with a little olive oil, cover, and rest **30 minutes**.
3. Gently sauté garlic in olive oil with chili flakes until fragrant (no browning).
4. Add a small ladle of pasta cooking water to the pan.
5. Add pasta, another ladle of water, season with salt.
6. Finish with parsley and Parmigiano. Toss until glossy.
