### Ingredients (2 servings)
- [ ] Onions — 800 g, thinly sliced
- [ ] Butter — 40 g
- [ ] Olive oil — 15 ml
- [ ] Beef or vegetable stock — 1 l
- [ ] Dry white wine — 100 ml
- [ ] Thyme — 2 sprigs
- [ ] Bay leaf — 1
- [ ] Gruyère or Comté — 120 g, grated
- [ ] Bread — 4 slices

---

### Preparation
1. Cook onions slowly in butter and olive oil until deeply golden.
2. Deglaze with white wine, reduce briefly.
3. Add stock, thyme, and bay leaf. Simmer 15–20 minutes.
4. Toast bread.
5. Ladle soup into bowls, add bread and cheese.
6. Broil until cheese is melted and browned.
