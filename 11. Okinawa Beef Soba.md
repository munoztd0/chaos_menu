### Ingredients (2 servings)

- [ ] Okinawa soba (long wheat noodles) — 200 g
- [ ] Beef (sirloin, ribeye, or chuck flap) — 250 g, very thinly sliced
- [ ] Soybean sprouts — 200 g
- [ ] Neutral oil — 20 ml
- [ ] Garlic — 2 cloves, finely grated
- [ ] Soy sauce — 2 tbsp
- [ ] Sugar — 1 tsp
- [ ] Beef or dashi broth — 500 ml
- [ ] Black pepper — 2–3 tsp, very coarsely ground

---

### Preparation
1. Bring broth to a gentle simmer. Add soy sauce and sugar.
2. Cook Okinawa soba in the broth until just tender.
3. Sear beef very quickly in hot oil with garlic; keep it pink and tender.
4. Blanch soybean sprouts briefly; drain well.
5. Assemble: noodles and broth first, beef on top, then a large handful of sprouts.
6. Finish with a heavy layer of very coarse black pepper.
